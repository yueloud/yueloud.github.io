Research

My research focuses on the formal verification of safety-critical software, with a particular emphasis on validating UML state machines. The formal tool I intend to use is the Cyclone online editor, and its feasibility has been well-established in the literature review. So far, I have completed the formal verification of OCL pre/post conditions using Cyclone. Two running examples, stack and queue, can be found in the sample code of the Cyclone online editor.
